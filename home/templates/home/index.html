Encourage Enrich Empower


Welcome to Doug Borrmann Christian Counseling. The mission of my practice is to Encourage you in the midst of your difficult seasons, to Enrich your life through education and customized strategies to achieve personal goals, and to Empower you with resources that will help you live each day to its maximum potential.


-Encourage-

Isaiah 35:1-4 God’s word promises that the “wasteland will rejoice and blossom” and that “wilderness and desert will be glad” once again. We are called to “strengthen those who have tired hands and encourage those who have weak knees.” We all need regular doses of encouragement and I place a high value on having a listening ear and providing an encouraging message to those who are in troubled places.

-Enrich-

Proverbs 8:11 Wisdom is truly more valuable than gold. It comes in many forms and from many sources. I believe the path of self-exploration is a place where wisdom is gained. Learning about yourself is an amazing way to fine tune your strengths and discover what will work best for you in your effort to be your best self. I will help you walk along the path of self-exploration and provide insights along the way.

-Empower-

Ephesians 6:13-17 We are given by God many resources that provide us with strength when we need it most. The armor of God is a metaphor that informs us to live in truth, righteousness, peace, faith, salvation, and with the Spirit. I believe we all have personal expressions for these resources and it is my goal to help you realize them so that when the storms of life come you will be empowered and prepared.
